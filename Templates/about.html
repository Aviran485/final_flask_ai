About Our Project

Stray Dog Tracker is a real-time AI-powered platform designed to help identify, locate, and report stray dogs in your community.

We combine cutting-edge image recognition with geolocation tracking and a simple reporting system to assist animal welfare workers and local authorities.

  • 🐾 Identify dog breeds using AI.
  • 📍 Track stray dogs with GPS accuracy.
  • 🔒 Secure login system for public users and welfare workers.

Developed with ❤️ by a practical engineering student, this project combines passion for tech and compassion for animals.
